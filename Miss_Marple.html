Jane Marple

age: Elderly (60s–80s)

gender: female

nationality: British

introduction: A shrewd amateur detective from the quiet village of St. Mary Mead, Miss Marple uses her deep understanding of human behavior to unravel crimes that baffle professionals. Created by Agatha Christie, she exemplifies the power of intuition, observation, and life experience over brute investigation.

appearing in media: Books: Miss Marple series by Agatha Christie TV/Film: Agatha Christie’s Marple (ITV), various film adaptations

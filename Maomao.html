Maomao

Age: 17

Gender: female

Nationality: Chinese-inspired fictional setting (Li Empire)

Introduction: Maomao is a sharp-witted apothecary who once lived in the pleasure district before being forced into service at the imperial palace. Her deep knowledge of medicine and poisons, combined with her insatiable curiosity, makes her a brilliant investigator of palace mysteries and medical incidents. Despite her modest origins, Maomao's intellect earns her recognition among the imperial elite.

Appearing in media: Light Novel/Manga/Anime: The Apothecary Diaries (Kusuriya no Hitorigoto)

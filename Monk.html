Adrian Monk

Age: 50s

Gender: male

Nationality: American

Introduction: Adrian Monk is a former San Francisco police detective who becomes a private consultant after the tragic death of his wife. Struggling with severe obsessive-compulsive disorder and multiple phobias, Monk uses his extraordinary observational skills and brilliant mind to solve complex cases that baffle others. His journey is as much about solving crimes as it is about coping with his personal challenges and seeking closure regarding his wife's death.

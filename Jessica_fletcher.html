Jessica Fletcher

Age: 50s-60s

Gender: Female

Nationality: American

Introduction: Jessica Fletcher is a retired English teacher turned mystery novelist and amateur detective, known for her sharp intellect and keen observational skills. Living in the small town of Cabot Cove, Maine, she often finds herself solving real-life murders that mirror her fiction. Warm, clever, and persistent, Jessica’s reputation for uncovering the truth precedes her, making her a beloved figure in the mystery world.

Appearing in media: Murder, She Wrote (TV series, 1984–1996), various Murder, She Wrote TV movies (1987–2003), cameo appearances in literature and pop culture references.

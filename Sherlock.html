Sherlock Holmes

Age: Varies (typically mid-30s to 40s)

Gender: Male

Nationality: British

Introduction: The world’s most famous consulting detective, Sherlock Holmes is renowned for his keen powers of observation, logical reasoning, and mastery of disguise. Created by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, Holmes solves the most perplexing crimes from his base at 221B Baker Street, London, often accompanied by his loyal friend Dr. John Watson. His analytical mind and eccentric personality make him an enduring icon of mystery fiction.

Appearing in media: Books: Sherlock Holmes series by Arthur Conan Doyle; TV/Film: Sherlock (BBC), Elementary, Sherlock Holmes (2009 film), Enola Holmes, and numerous adaptations.

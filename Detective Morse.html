Detective Endeavour Morse

Age: 20s–50s (varies by series)

Gender: male

Nationality: British

Introduction: Inspector Morse is a cultured, melancholic detective with a love for classical music, literature, and ale. Working in Oxford, he relies on intuition and intellect rather than forensic evidence. Known for his complex personality and moral depth, Morse’s investigations often reveal the darker sides of human nature.

Appearing in media: TV: Inspector Morse, Lewis (spin-off), Endeavour (prequel)

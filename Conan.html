Conan Edogawa

Age: 6–7 (physically), 17 (mentally; real name Shinichi Kudo)

Gender: male

Nationality: Japanese

Introduction: Once a brilliant teenage detective, Shinichi Kudo is transformed into a child after being poisoned by a shadowy organization. Under the alias Conan Edogawa, he continues to solve crimes while searching for a cure and battling the mysterious Black Organization. Despite his childlike appearance, Conan's sharp intellect and deductive skills remain unparalleled.

Kotoko Iwanaga

Age: 17

Gender: female

Nationality: Japanese

Introduction: Kotoko Iwanaga is a young woman who acts as the “Goddess of Wisdom” for supernatural beings after losing one leg and one eye in exchange for her power. She mediates between humans and yōkai, using her intelligence and sharp reasoning to solve cases involving the paranormal. Her partnership—and romance—with Kuro Sakuragawa adds emotional depth to her unusual detective work.

Appearing in media: Anime/Manga/Light Novel: In/Spectre (Kyokou Suiri)
